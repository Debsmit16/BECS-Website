Last updated: January 2024

At Banerjee Electronics and Consultancy Services (BECS), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:
